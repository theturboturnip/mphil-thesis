\chapter{Background}

This chapter describes RISC-V (\cref{chap:bg:sec:riscv}), RVV (\cref{chap:bg:sec:rvv}), and CHERI (\cref{chap:bg:sec:cheri}) to the detail required to understand the rest of the dissertation.
It summarizes the relevant sections of the RISC-V unprivileged spec\todocite{}, the RISC-V ``V'' extension specification v1.0\todocite[Sections 1-9, 17]{}, the TR-951 CHERI ISAv8 technical report\todocite[Chapters 5, 8]{TR-951}, and the TR-949 technical report about C/C++ safety on CHERI\todocite[Section 4.4, Appendix C]{TR-949}.
Both vectors and CHERI are described, because this dissertation caters to those who may be familiar with one but not the other.
